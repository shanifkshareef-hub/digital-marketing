Know Us Better

We craft digital strategies that deliver impact.

Back in 2021, a few of us in our early 20’s, young, ambitious, and passionate about creating, Decided to stop waiting for the “right time” and just start. LA Creatives was born as our entry into concerts and event media production. We built our reputation through social media and word of mouth, earning trust by working with A list artists and some of the biggest names in the country. Shortly after, we launched The After Productions in our passion to capture the purest moments of love and transforming them into beautiful, cinematic stories that live on forever.. Over the years, we have explored multiple niches, from automotive and food to luxury resorts, events, lifestyle, and travel, constantly evolving, adapting, and honing our craft with every project. From long nights to packed shoot days, Everything led us here. Today, with The Creative Souq LLC, we proudly carry forward that legacy in the Middle East, driven by the same ambition we had in the beginning. And We’re only getting started.
